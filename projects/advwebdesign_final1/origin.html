Origin

The History of My Family



My family came from humble roots in the villages and towns of Chittagong, Bangladesh, a small place in a small country. But, my grandparents worked hard to provide good educations for my parents, so that they could grow up to be successful and one day, move to the city. Over time, my father moved to the city and there, he married my mother. And, that's where I grew up.

Spreading Out into the World



Over the decades, my family has spread out across the world in search of a better life for the next generation. The first to go were my aunts, who immigrated to India with their families. My uncle and his family followed them to India soon after. Then, my father won the Diversity Visa (DV) lottery and got visas for my mother and I to move to the US. And, that's where I have lived ever since.

In the Present



Most of my family from both father's side and my mother's side are in either the US or India. The thousands of miles of land and water between us are deafening. We always look forward to the annual celebrations, like Durga Puja that reunite the Ghose family. My parents and I may be content with our lives in America, but we are always a bit happier when the rest of family is around.
