About Me

Aspiring Product Manager based in New York

Hi! My name is Prangon Ghose and I am an engineer with a passion for product management and innovative design. I am currently a second year student at Stony Brook University pursuing a degree in Computer Engineering with a minor in History. I aspire towards a career that will allow me to channel my technical skills and my passion for the liberal arts to create products with seamless user experiences and incredible functionality.

Currently, I am the Software Team Lead for the Stony Brook Robotics Team, SBU's official competition robotics team. In addition, I serve as the Lead Content and Social Media Management Intern at the Center for Civic Justice, a non-partisan civic engagement focused non-profit at SBU. I am also a Mentor in the CEAS Peer Mentoring Program, a program designed to help acclimate incoming SBU engineering students to life on campus.

In my free time, I enjoy taking meditative walks, reading history books and eating butter pecan ice-cream.
