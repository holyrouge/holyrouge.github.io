About me

Hi! I am Prangon, an aspiring engineer from New York City. I currently serve as the Director of Logistics for StuyHacks, an organization that hosts hackathons for highschoolers in the Greater New York Area. As a student at Stuyvesant High School in New York, NY, I have had the opportunity to dive into the world of computer science (CS) through multiple technology classes and various clubs. Through StuyHacks, I hope to provide more students with the opportunity to learn about programming and connect them with fellow coders. My aim is to enrich my learning experience through internship opportunities and projects with fellow members of the CS community.
